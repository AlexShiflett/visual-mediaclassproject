My Fall 2018 Semester

Housing and The Consumer

Ms. Wimmer

This class has examples of how homes differ and how you should look into buying them. It is an online class and is a part of my minor. The class has a decent work load with 2 to 3 assignments a week on top of the weekly videos and readings. The class teaches you about different home styles and how they can differ depending on their builds. Going into the class I knew very little about this subject, but I have learned a large amount so far and can use it for when I buy my first home. I recommend this class to any students who are looking to rent or buy a home.
